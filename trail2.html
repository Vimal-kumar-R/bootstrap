About this

Goal 17 is about revitalizing the global partnership for sustainable development. The 2030 Agenda is universal and calls for action by all countries – developed and developing – to ensure no one is left behind. It requires partnerships between governments, the private sector, and civil society

For sustainable development to be achieved, it is crucial to harmonize three core elements: economic growth, social inclusion and environmental protection. These elements are interconnected and all are crucial for the well-being of individuals and societies

Stronger partnerships will contribute to environmental protection and sustainable development by mobilizing resources, sharing knowledge, promoting the creation and transfer of environmentally sound technologies, and building capacit

The main aim is to achieve global sustainable development. The plan was to fight against environmental damage, diseases and poverty. This can be achieved through global cooperation on mutual needs, common interests and shared responsibilities

Solution

Sustainable development can be achieved through the following ways


  1. Improvement in human capacity is needed through better education and healthcare that will result in improving the quality of life and better decisions related to the environment.
  2. Consumption and production should be managed responsibly so as to avoid wastage
  3. Decarbonise the energy system so as to provide clean and affordable energy to all.
  4. Providing access to clean drinking water and nutritious food.
  5. Planning smart infrastructure so that the population can be managed properly
  6. Responsible use of information technology to support sustainable development.
